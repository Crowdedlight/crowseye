STAHP Loan Program Guide

Summary

The STAHP Loan Program is designed to empower newbros into ISK making through daytripping. Ships are provided for daytrips, and the resulting ISK profit is split 60/40 between the newbie and the loan program, in the newbie's favor. Ideally, the newbie will be able to purchase his own ship within 2-3 trips.

Rules

1. Any newbie can apply to receive a loaner ship at the beginning of a fleet by contacting the FC.

2. The newbie is not charged anything up front, and is not charged anything for the loss of the loaner boat or anything at all until loot is acquired

3. The newbie will be flagged on the loot sheet so that 25% of his cut will be given to the loan program and STAHP SRP

4. A newbie may be banned from receiving a loaner boat in the future if he loses it by not following FC orders and/or otherwise being entirely too dumb too live.

5, The newbie must fulfil the basic skill requirements for sitting in the boat to receive a loan.

6. The boat must be returned at the end of the fleet.
